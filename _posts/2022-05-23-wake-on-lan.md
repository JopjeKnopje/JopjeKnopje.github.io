So I had this laptop laying around that I setup as a server to sync files between my laptop and pc.  
But having it running 24/7 seems like a waste.  
I deceided to use WakeOnLan,  Which the motherboard didn't support.  
No problem just use an esp8266 running a [Telegram Bot](https://core.telegram.org/bots) switching a relay wired to the power button.
